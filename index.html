About us

Our Mission & Vision

Anant Manzil is an initiative started by youth taken to provide help and support to needy one. Considering the poor plight of living beings in India, we aim to eradicate social evils as much possible as it is. Elimination of problem is difficult but we can start reducing its magnitude for which we are working. It is a social cause and as you know this saying “sirf humare karne se kya hota hai” let’s prove this wrong and let’s say “hum sabke karne se hoga” If we'll pool our efforts we'll surely make a big difference. Volunteers are the only human beings on the face of the earth who reflect this nation’s compassion, unselfish caring, patience, and just plain loving one another.

How did it start?

It is initiated by college students. Students from different colleges, different cities, different cultures came up with a idea of doing doing something for the people who really need help. It all started with a phone call and the idea of joining an NGO for social work. But we came to know that for volunteering we need to pay. Then we decided to commence something that will provide a platform to youth to come up with ideas initiate projects. Youth from different states came up with different problems in their region. Anant Manzil is an NGO that will provide platform and resources to the youth who have ideas but have limited resources..
